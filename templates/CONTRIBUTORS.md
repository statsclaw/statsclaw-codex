| Tier | Requirement | Badge |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Contributor | 1+ entries | brain |
| Bronze | 5+ entries | brain x5 |
| Silver | 15+ entries | brain x15 |
| Gold | 30+ entries | brain x30 |

## How to Earn Badges

1. Use StatsClaw with Brain mode enabled
2. When a workflow produces noteworthy knowledge, StatsClaw's distiller agent extracts it
3. You review and approve the extracted knowledge before it's shared
4. Your contribution is submitted as a PR to [statsclaw/brain-seedbank](https://github.com/statsclaw/brain-seedbank)
5. Admin reviews and transfers accepted entries to this repo
6. Your badge count is updated in the leaderboard above

All contributions are public and attributed to your GitHub username.
